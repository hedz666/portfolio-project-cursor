## Selection Criteria

Experts were chosen based on:
1. **Practitioner status** — They build tools, run agencies, or execute
   AI SEO campaigns, not just write about them
2. **Recency** — Active publication in 2025–2026 on AI-specific SEO topics
3. **Methodological depth** — They publish frameworks, data, or processes
   that can be replicated, not just opinions
4. **Complementary coverage** — Together, these 10 cover the full AI SEO
   content production lifecycle: strategy (Indig), entity optimization
   (Barnard, Jones), content quality (Ray, Haynes), technical NLP (King,
   Gübür), distribution/GEO (Simmonds), workflows (McDowell), and
   international/structural SEO (Solis)
