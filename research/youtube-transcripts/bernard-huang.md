## Key takeaways (quick scan)

- **AEO vs SEO core difference**: SEO targets keywords users type into Google; AEO targets what agents are thinking when creating responses. You fight for being part of the answer, not ranking in 10 blue links.
- **How AI constructs responses — 3 layers**: Training data (slow, knowledge cutoff), Research & validation (AI web searches to prevent hallucinations), Memory & context (past conversations). Brands can influence layer 1 and layer 2.
- **Off-page AEO shift**: Off-page SEO was 80% backlink focused; off-page AEO is 60% backlinks + 20-30% brand mentions. Unlinked brand mentions now carry significant weight.
- **Prompt tracking replaces rank tracking**: Use monthly search volume as a proxy for prompt volume. Track whether AI cites your pages and what web searches agents perform during research.
- **Entity relevance over keyword stuffing**: AI relies on topical authority and entity co-occurrences — not user engagement signals — for low-search-volume AI web searches. Clear Scope surfaces which entities AI algorithms look for.
- **Brand sentiment matters**: Ask ChatGPT/Gemini what they think about your brand, identify weaknesses, then update cited pages to defend against negative associations.
- **AI citations compound**: Getting cited by AI likely qualifies your content for future training data updates — creating a compounding visibility loop.
- **Quick start AEO playbook**: Understand AI topic model → identify prompt clusters → create entity-rich content targeting agent web searches → publish → track citations → improve brand sentiment on cited pages.
