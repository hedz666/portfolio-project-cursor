## Key takeaways (quick scan)

- **AI recommendations can convert better**: claims customers from AI recommendations are **4.4×** more valuable than typical Google clicks.
- **AI SEO ≠ traditional SEO**:
  - **Less keyword-phrase obsession**; AI “works it out” more like a human.
  - **Personalization matters**: AI models know more context about the searcher (location, prior conversations, etc.).
  - **Conversational writing wins**: plain language, “human-sounding” content.
  - **Third-person, expert tone** often performs better than first-person sales copy.
  - **AI tools care less about “duplicate/thin” content** than Google (focus on being helpful, specific, brief).
- **Target bottom-of-funnel buyer intent**: train AI to pick you as “best *[service]* in *[category/city]*”.
- **On-page playbook (website content to add)**:
  - **Best-fit briefs**: short “who’s the best X?” posts for specific situations.
  - **Top reasons** posts: “Top reasons to choose [business] for [service]”.
  - **Service × city posts**: “Best [service] in [city] — [business]”.
  - **Comparison tables** vs competitors (structured, decision-friendly).
  - Core pages: **FAQ**, **Case Studies**, **Testimonials**, **Pricing**.
- **Off-page playbook (signals around the web)**:
  - Get mentioned broadly (directories/profiles); **mentions > backlinks** (for AI tools).
  - Collect **more 5-star reviews** (Yelp highlighted as influential).
  - Get included in “best of” lists / roundups; press releases can amplify mentions.
